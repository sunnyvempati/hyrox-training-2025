# London Travel Week - Maintenance Phase
*November 12-16, 2025*
*10 days to race - Stay sharp, manage jet lag*

## **Travel Considerations**
- 6-hour time difference (London ahead)
- Limited equipment access
- Jet lag management critical
- Maintain without building fatigue

---

## **Tuesday Nov 12 - Travel Day**
- Hydrate aggressively on plane
- Walk airport terminals
- Light stretching at hotel
- Early to bed London time

## **Wednesday Nov 13 - Jet Lag Shakeout**
*Time: 30 min*
- 20 min easy run @ 8:00-8:30/mile
- 5 x 100m strides
- Stretch
- **Do this in morning London time to reset clock**

## **Thursday Nov 14 - Race Pace Touch**
*Time: 35 min*
- 10 min warm-up jog
- 3 x 1000m @ 7:00-7:10/mile (race pace)
- 2 min rest between
- 10 min cool-down
- **Find a park or treadmill**

## **Friday Nov 15 - Hotel Gym Circuit**
*Time: 30 min*
- 10 min bike/row warm-up
- 3 rounds:
  - 20 burpees
  - 30 wall balls (or thrusters)
  - 400m row (or 2 min bike)
  - 2 min rest
- Stay controlled, just moving

## **Saturday Nov 16 - Return Travel Day**
- Airport walks
- Hydrate
- Light stretching

## **Sunday Nov 17 - Recovery Day**
- Complete rest OR
- 20 min easy walk/jog if feeling good
- Focus on getting back on home timezone

---

## **Key Points**
- Nothing hard or long
- Use morning workouts to combat jet lag
- Pack resistance bands for hotel room
- Find parks for running (Hyde Park, Regent's Park)
- Don't stress if you miss a session
- Priority is arriving home feeling good

**Race is 6 days after return - plenty of time to readjust!**
