### **Station Strategy**

**Ski Erg (Station 1)**
- Steady rhythm, not sprint
- Target: 4:30-4:45

**Sled Push (Station 2)**
- Short, powerful steps
- Don't stop mid-push
- Target: 3:00-3:15

**Sled Pull (Station 3)**
- Use your proven technique
- Steady pulls, don't rush
- Target: 7:00-7:30

**Burpee Box Jump (Station 4)**
- Consistent rhythm over speed
- Step down if needed to save legs
- Target: 5:45-6:00
- **FUEL HERE:** Gel after this station

**Row (Station 5)**
- Strong, controlled strokes
- Don't empty the tank
- Target: 4:30-4:45

**Farmer's Carry (Station 6)**
- Pick a landmark, don't drop weights
- Quick but controlled
- Target: 2:15-2:30

**Lunges (Station 7)**
- Steady rhythm, don't rush
- Good form over speed
- Target: 4:30-4:45

**Wall Balls (Station 8)**
- **YOUR PLAN:** 5 sets of 20 reps
- Quick 5-second breaks
- Don't go for hero sets
- Target: 4:45-5:00
