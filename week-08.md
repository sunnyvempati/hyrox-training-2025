## **Tuesday - Sled Power + Speed**
*Time Target: 65 minutes*
**Back to aggressive sled work**

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 800m progressive jog
- Movement prep: squats, lunges, arm swings

### Main Set (45 min)
**SLED POWER:** (25 min)
1. **Sled Push:** 10 x 25m @ heavy load (300lbs+)
   - Focus: Maximum power output
   - Rest: 75s between sets

2. **Sled Pull:** 10 x 25m @ moderate-heavy load (250lbs)
   - Focus: Speed and efficiency
   - Rest: 75s between sets

*5 min transition*

**Speed Circuit:** (15 min)
3 rounds for time:
- 300m ski erg (all out)
- 15 burpee box jumps (speed focus)
- 20 wall balls (unbroken)
- Rest: 2 min between rounds

**Target:** Sub-4:30 per round

**Cool-down:** 10 min walk + stretching

---

## **Wednesday - Threshold Domination**
*Time Target: 70 minutes*
**Push your threshold power**

### Warm-up (12 min)
- 8 min progressive jog
- 4 x 100m strides with full recovery

### Main Set
**Threshold Intervals:** 8 x 600m @ 6:10-6:20/mile pace
- Your sweet spot based on previous performances
- Rest: 75s between intervals
- Cool-down: 10 min easy

*8 min transition*

**Lower Body Power:** (20 min)
Choose 1 exercise from each group:

**Quad Dominant:** 4 sets x 15 reps
- Options: Goblet squats, front squats, leg press

**Hip Dominant:** 4 sets x 12 reps each leg
- Options: Bulgarian split squats, single-leg RDLs, step-ups

**Posterior Chain:** 4 sets x 15 reps
- Options: Hip thrusts, glute bridges, reverse lunges

**Explosive:** 3 sets x 10 reps
- Options: Jump squats, box jumps, bounds

**Cool-down:** 10 min walk + stretching

**RPE Target:** 9/10 (hardest day of week)
